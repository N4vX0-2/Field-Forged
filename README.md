# Field Forged
**Autonomia Libertas Secretum Integritas**

**Privacy-first, offline job estimation built for the trades.**

> Field Forged is a privacy-first, offline-ready job estimation tool designed for independent tradespeople and small field service teams.  
> Built for speed, autonomy, and full ownership — no cloud, no subscriptions, no compromise.  
> Your work, your data, your rules. Unlike cloud platforms that track, profile, and lock you out, Field Forged gives you total control with zero surveillance, zero lock-in, and zero legal traps.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Labor & Materials Tracking
- Assign labor hours and crew sizes to line items
- Input materials and itemized costs on the fly
- Real-time summary of total job cost, billable rate, and margin

### Fast, light-weight, Offline Estimate Building
- No huge applicatiion that freezes
- Completely offline, runs in-browser
- No sign-in, no sync, no stored customer data
- Build and print/send professional estimates in minutes

### Field-Ready Templates
- Service call estimates
- HVAC system replacement comparisons
- Mechanical, plumbing, electrical line-item jobs
- Thermal audit + energy modeling quote support

### Flexible Billing & Payback Tools
- Simple payback calculators
- Net cost vs. annual savings summary
- Quote builder integrated with cost/efficiency models

### Locally-Based Storage
- All data stored only on your device/browser unless **YOU** move it
- JSON export/import options for portability
- Equipment libraries, findings, and job records all local

## Built For

Field Forged is designed for **any individual or small team that works in a trade profession**, particularly where jobs involve **time-based labor, material usage, and service quoting**.

**Ideal for:**
- HVAC technicians
- Electricians
- Plumbers
- General contractors
- Appliance repair techs
- Energy auditors
- Handypeople and solo operators
- Small crews offering residential or light commercial services
- Inspectors and estimators

**Also suitable for industries needing:**
- Time + material tracking
- Line-item scope writing
- Photo-based documentation
- Local-first quoting tools

Whether you’re replacing a panel, quoting a ductless install, or inspecting thermal loss in a crawlspace — **Field Forged provides the toolkit**.
